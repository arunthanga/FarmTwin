KrishiTwin Farm Digital Twin · MVP

A simulation-driven twin of a 15-acre rain-shadow farm in Eruthempathy (Chittur, Palakkad). The field is discretized into a mesh of irrigation zones; a daily water-balance + crop-growth solver steps each zone forward to recommend precise irrigation. Compare flood vs uniform-drip vs twin-optimized scheduling and see water saved at protected yield.

Scenario controls

Mesh: 16 x 10 = 160 zones · root-zone water balance with lateral redistribution · season length 90 days. Numbers are agronomic estimates for demo/pitch use.
