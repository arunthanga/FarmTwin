# FarmTwin — Pitch Script (synced with `pitch-deck.md` / `pitch-deck.html`)

Use for KSUM Idea Grant pitch day, AgriNext Summit, and GAAM Demo Day. ~5–7 minutes.

---

## 1. Open — the canal (30 sec)

> "Water just arrived in Chittur. The Moolathara canal extension finally delivers irrigation to three and a half thousand hectares in Kerala's rain-shadow belt. But farmers still irrigate by calendar and habit — not by what the crop and soil actually need. **Scheduling is now the bottleneck.**"

## 2. Problem — quantified pain (45 sec)

> "Over-irrigation wastes thirty to fifty percent of scarce canal water. Mistimed irrigation costs ten to twenty-five percent of yield. Fifteen hundred vegetable farmers across six hundred fifty hectares have no plot-level, day-by-day tool — and FPOs have no cluster water budget."

## 3. Solution — the twin (60 sec)

> "**FarmTwin** is a farm digital twin. We discretize the field into a **mesh** of zones — the same technique as finite-element meshing — and run a daily **water-balance and crop-growth solver**. The twin answers: irrigate or not, how many litres, where, today — and why. Farmers can run what-if scenarios — flood versus drip versus optimized, heat wave, dry spell — *before* acting in the field."

## 4. Demo — show the MVP (90 sec)

> *[Open `mvp/index.html`, click "Run full-season comparison"]*
>
> "On a modelled fifteen-acre layout, twin-optimized scheduling saves roughly thirty percent water versus flood baseline at equal or better yield. This is not a dashboard — it's a calibrated solver. The headline number you see here is what we're validating on my farm this season."

## 5. Moat — CAE credibility (45 sec)

> "I've spent twenty years meshing and simulating physical systems — crash models, assembly lines. A farm is just another domain to mesh and solve. And unlike most agritech founders, **I own the test rig** — a fifteen-acre farm inside the target cluster."

## 6. GTM — FPO beachhead + GAAM scale (60 sec)

> "We enter through **FPOs** in the Chittur cluster — one sale reaches hundreds of farmers, and KERA AgriNext routes funding and adoption through FPOs. Once the pilot is proven and we're KSUM-registered, Kerala's **Government as a Marketplace** policy lets any department buy from us **up to fifty lakh rupees, exclusive of GST, without a tender**. A district Krishi Bhavan cluster deployment fits that band perfectly. **AgriNext proves the product; GAAM scales it.**"

## 7. Ask (30 sec)

> "We're asking for the **Idea Grant** to instrument and calibrate the pilot farm, and **AgriNext** introductions to match with a Chittur FPO and Agriculture Department buyers for the GAAM pathway. Attachments: working MVP, field pilot plan, and FPO MoU template ready to sign."

## 8. Close (15 sec)

> "**FarmTwin** — mesh the field, solve the schedule, protect the yield. Thank you."
