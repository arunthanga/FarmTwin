This is personal

In my village, Eruthempathy, farmers run out of water.

  • We get < 1,000 mm rain/yr — one-third of Kerala's average.
  • Neighbours queue for hours at the regulator for a tanker's worth of drinking water.
  • The young leave, because the soil can't pay them back.

"I spent 20 years simulating the world's factories — but my own village couldn't predict whether water would reach a field next week."

The problem (the head, after the heart)

  • Palakkad's east is Kerala's driest belt; water is contested (shared with Tamil Nadu) & badly distributed.
  • 26 blocks in Palakkad rated unsafe on groundwater.
  • The old Moolathara lift canal: no summer water for 7 years → ~1,000 acres of coconut hit.
  • A ₹2-cr clean-up was announced — and nothing happened.

Building canals ≠ delivering water to roots. The last mile is an information & control problem, not a concrete one.

Why now money is flowing into our backyard

  • Moolathara Right Bank Canal Extension — KIIFB, ~₹262 cr, Kerala's largest community micro-irrigation project, commissioning early 2026.
  • Serves 3,575 ha (Phase I) & 10,000+ ha (Phase II) — naming Eruthempathy, Vadakarapathy, Kozhinjampara.
  • CMI is an active, repeating pipeline: ~₹40.5 cr already tendered across 3 zones.
  • Farmers get up to 85% subsidy (PDMC + Kerala Samrudhi).

Capital is subsidised. The missing piece is the intelligence layer — that's us.

The solution: FarmTwin

A self-calibrating digital twin for irrigation — two products, one learning engine.

FarmTwin Studio

BEFORE install

Survey land → solver optimises the network (pumps, pipes, valves, drips, sensors, fertigation) → best 2-3 layouts by cost, energy, uniformity, yield.

FarmTwin Runtime

AFTER install

Solar, wireless edge controllers read sensors + weather + cloud → decide when/how long to irrigate & fertigate; act on pumps & valves; reject bad data.

Underneath: FarmTwin Engine — hydraulic + FAO-56 agronomy solver whose parameters keep learning from the field.

Same discipline as CAE: discretise → solve the physics → compare to reality → correct. Most agritech stops at dashboards. We predict, optimise & control — and get smarter every farm.

Business model (subsidy-aligned)

  1. Design & optimisation (Studio): project fee ~₹3-6k/ha.
  2. Digital-twin + IoT (Runtime): hardware + software — fundable inside the ~85% micro-irrigation subsidy.
  3. Recurring SaaS + O&M / performance monitoring: ~₹1,000-1,500/ha/yr — sticky, scalable, fixes the "nobody maintained it" failure.
  4. Phase 2: data-driven agri-fintech (yield-backed credit, insurance).

Go-to-market

  1. Prove — 15 acres in Eruthempathy + one Moolathara CMI zone (KSUM pilot).
  2. Win — Moolathara design / monitoring with a civil partner.
  3. Expand — Chitturpuzha command area + Palakkad FPOs.
  4. Replicate — KIIFB-CMI statewide (Kerala) & PDMC nationally.
  5. Globalise — software/twin (near-zero marginal cost).
