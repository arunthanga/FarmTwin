# FarmTwin — One-Pager

**Simulation-driven farm digital twin · Eruthempathy, Chittur (Palakkad)**

---

## One-line pitch

FarmTwin builds a simulation-driven digital twin of a farm — a mesh of zones where each cell models soil moisture, evapotranspiration, nutrients and crop growth — so farmers and FPOs irrigate and fertilize by precise need instead of habit, cutting water/input use 30–50% while protecting yield.

## Problem

Kerala's rain-shadow belt receives ~1/3 of state rainfall. The 2025 Moolathara canal extension delivers water to ~3,575 ha, but farmers still irrigate by calendar. Result: 30–50% water wasted, 10–25% yield lost, and no plot-level decision tool for this microclimate. FPOs across 650+ ha lack cluster water budgeting.

## Solution

Discretize the field into a **computational mesh**; run a **water-balance + crop-growth solver** daily; optimize irrigation/fertigation schedules. Hardware-light SaaS — IP is the simulation engine. Working MVP at `mvp/index.html`.

## Differentiation

Predicts and optimizes (true digital twin), not a sensor dashboard. Founder: 20 years CAE/simulation (meshing, assembly-line sim) + owned 15-acre pilot farm.
