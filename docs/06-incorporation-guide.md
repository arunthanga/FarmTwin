# 06 — Incorporation Guide (Kerala)

KSUM grants require incorporation only **before disbursal**, but DPIIT and the
Seed Fund need it, so incorporate once the Idea Grant is shortlisted (or earlier
if you want DPIIT benefits immediately).

## Pvt Ltd vs LLP vs OPC — decision

| Factor | Private Limited | LLP | OPC |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Raise equity / VC, ESOPs | Yes (best) | No | No |
| KSUM Seed Fund eligible | Yes | Yes | Limited |
| KSUM Scale-up Seed Fund | **Required (Pvt Ltd only)** | No | No |
| Compliance cost | Higher | Lower | Medium |
| Multiple founders | Yes | Yes | No (single) |

**Recommendation: Private Limited Company.** It is the only structure eligible
for the full KSUM funding ladder including the Scale-up Seed Fund (Rs.25 lakh),
and it is what VCs and angels expect. LLP only makes sense if you are certain you
will never raise equity. OPC is a fallback only if you incorporate strictly solo
and want minimal compliance — but it blocks Scale-up funding, so avoid it.

This also informs the open question from the plan: if you can bring in even one
co-founder/CTO or technical employee, Pvt Ltd is clearly right. If a woman holds
the majority share, you additionally unlock the **Women Productisation Grant
(up to Rs.12 lakh extra)** — worth considering in the cap-table design.

## Incorporation steps (Pvt Ltd)  [ACTION]

1. **Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)** for each director.
2. **Director Identification Number (DIN)** — obtained via the SPICe+ form.
3. **Name reservation** via SPICe+ Part A on the MCA portal
   (<https://www.mca.gov.in/>). Proposed names (in order):
   - KrishiTwin Technologies Private Limited
   - KrishiTwin Agri Intelligence Private Limited
   - (keep 2 backups; avoid names too close to existing companies/trademarks)
4. **SPICe+ Part B**: company details, registered office (the Eruthempathy
   address or a Palakkad office), capital, directors, subscribers.
5. File linked forms: **eMoA (INC-33), eAoA (INC-34), AGILE-PRO-S** (GST, EPFO,
   ESIC, bank account, professional tax).
6. Receive **Certificate of Incorporation (COI)**, company **PAN** and **TAN**.
7. **File INC-22A (ACTIVE)** so MCA status is "Active/Active Compliant" — KSUM
   Seed Fund explicitly checks this.
8. Open the **company current account** (needed for grant disbursal).

### Keep registered office in Kerala
KSUM schemes require the startup to be registered **in Kerala**. Use a Kerala
address (the farm/Palakkad). This also strengthens the "local, on-the-ground"
narrative for AgriNext.

## Suggested initial cap table (illustrative)
- Founder (you): majority.
- Reserve an **ESOP pool (~10%)** for an early technical/agronomy hire.
- Optionally allocate shares so a woman co-founder holds majority **if** you want
  the Women Productisation Grant — a deliberate, optional choice.

## Post-incorporation checklist
- [ ] COI + PAN + TAN received
- [ ] INC-22A (ACTIVE) filed -> MCA status "Active Compliant"
- [ ] Company current account opened
- [ ] DPIIT recognition applied (doc 03 Part B)
- [ ] Registered office in Kerala confirmed
- [ ] GST registration (from AGILE-PRO-S) if required

## Rough cost / time
- Government + professional fees: ~Rs.8,000-15,000 (DSC, stamp duty, CA/CS).
- Timeline: typically 7-15 working days end to end.
- Budgeted under the Idea Grant "IP/legal" line (doc 04).
