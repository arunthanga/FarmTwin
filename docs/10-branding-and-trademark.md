# 10 — Branding & Trademark Due Diligence

## Decision

- **Platform / company brand:** **FarmTwin**
- **Product 1 (pre-install design + optimization):** **FarmTwin Studio**
- **Product 2 (runtime control + digital twin + fertigation):** **FarmTwin Runtime**
- **Shared engine / core:** **FarmTwin Engine** (Python package identifier kept as
  `FarmTwin` internally to avoid breaking imports; brand-facing name is FarmTwin Engine)

> Status: **chosen with a known, accepted risk** (see "FarmTwin" row below). A formal
> trademark clearance search by a registered TM agent is still recommended before filing.

## Name-availability check (informal web search, not a legal clearance)

| **FieldTwin** | **FutureOn / Kongsberg Digital** product; established digital-twin SaaS, even split into "FieldTwin Design" + "FieldTwin Operate". [ref](https://www.futureon.com/product/design) | Avoided — high conflict |
| **CropTwin** | (1) **Croptwin Agro Tech Pvt Ltd**, registered Indian company (TN, CIN U46101TZ2024PTC032728, Oct 2024); (2) USDA/Nebraska "CropTwin" irrigation digital-twin research. [ref](https://www.neusourcestartup.com/companies/croptwin-agro-tech-private-limited) | Avoided — highest conflict in India |
| **FarmTwin** (chosen) | Used by a **Spanish government-funded R&D project** for **pig-farm** digital twins (farmtwin.es). Different sub-sector (livestock vs. irrigation) and geography. [ref](http://farmtwin.es/) | **Chosen, risk accepted** |

### Why FarmTwin is workable despite the Spanish project
- Different field of use (irrigation/crop hydraulics vs. porcine facility management) and
  different territory (EU project vs. India), which reduces likelihood-of-confusion for an
  Indian trademark in the relevant classes.
- It is a **project**, not (so far as found) a registered Indian trademark.

### Residual risk
- "FarmTwin" is descriptive ("farm" + "twin"), so it is a **weaker mark** and harder to
  enforce. Consider always pairing it with a distinctive logo/wordmark and the product
  suffixes (Studio / Runtime) to strengthen distinctiveness.

## Required next steps (before relying on the name)

1. **Formal TM search** at IP India ([tmrsearch.ipindia.gov.in/ESEARCH](https://tmrsearch.ipindia.gov.in/ESEARCH);
   note the portal migration on 1 Jul 2026) across:
   - **Class 9** (software), **Class 42** (SaaS/PaaS, design services),
   - **Class 7 / 11** (pumps, valves, irrigation hardware — for the controller/node).
2. **MCA name check** (RUN / SPICe+) for the legal entity — the Pvt Ltd name may differ
   from the brand (e.g., *FarmTwin Technologies Private Limited*). See doc 06.
3. **File TM-A** for the wordmark + logo (DPIIT-recognised startups get the reduced fee).
4. **Secure assets:** `farmtwin.in` / `.com` / `.ai` domains and social handles.
5. **Fallback shortlist** if clearance fails: distinctive parents **Pravaha** (Sanskrit
   *flow*), **Sahya** (Western Ghats), or **Vrishti** (*rainfall*), each with the same
   Studio / Runtime product structure.

> Disclaimer: the availability findings above are from public web searches, not a legal
> trademark clearance opinion. Confirm with a registered trademark agent/attorney.
