# 14 — Partnership MoU + Vendor-Registration Checklist

> Purpose: be ready to bid for the **next** Moolathara RBC / CMI packages (Phase-II zones,
> Phase-I O&M, DPR/monitoring work) and similar KIIFB-CMI / PDMC micro-irrigation tenders.
>
> FarmTwin is a software + digital-twin company. We do **not** do civil/earthworks. To win
> government micro-irrigation work we either (a) **partner** with an eligible civil
> contractor / system integrator and supply the *intelligence layer* (design optimisation,
> digital twin, IoT control, performance monitoring), or (b) register as a **specialist
> vendor / sub-consultant** for design, instrumentation and O&M-monitoring scopes.
>
> ⚠️ **This is a working template, not legal advice.** Have a Kerala-licensed advocate /
> CS review before signing anything, and verify every tender's eligibility clauses against
> the actual NIT/RFP. Figures and clause names below are placeholders to be filled in.

## Part A — Memorandum of Understanding (template)

**MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING**

This MoU is made on **____ [date]** at **____ [place]**, between:

1. **[FarmTwin entity name]**, a [private limited company / LLP / startup] incorporated
   under the Companies Act 2013, having its registered office at **[address],
   Eruthempathy, Chittur, Palakkad, Kerala**, represented by **[Founder name], [designation]**
   (hereinafter **"FarmTwin"** / the **"Technology Partner"**); and

2. **[Civil contractor / integrator name]**, a [company/firm] having its office at
   **[address]**, with [PWD / Irrigation Dept / KIIDC] contractor registration **Class ___**,
   represented by **[name], [designation]** (hereinafter the **"Lead/EPC Partner"**).

FarmTwin and the Lead Partner are individually a **"Party"** and collectively the **"Parties"**.

### 1. Purpose
The Parties wish to collaborate, on a non-exclusive basis (subject to clause 9), to jointly
pursue and execute **community micro-irrigation (CMI) and precision-irrigation works** under
the **Moolathara Right Bank Canal Extension** and related KIIFB / KIIDC / WRD / PMKSY-PDMC
programmes, combining the Lead Partner's civil-works eligibility and the Technology Partner's
design-optimisation, digital-twin, IoT-control and performance-monitoring capabilities.

### 2. Scope split
| Workstream | Responsible Party |
| --- | --- |
| Tendering eligibility (class, turnover, EMD/PBG), civil & pipeline execution, statutory site obligations | **Lead Partner** |
| Survey-driven network **design & optimisation** (FarmTwin Studio): pump/pipe/valve/emitter/sensor layout, BoM, hydraulic + FAO-56 sizing | **FarmTwin** |
| **Digital-twin + IoT** supply & commissioning (FarmTwin Runtime): controllers, sensors, fertigation control, telemetry | **FarmTwin** |
| **Performance monitoring & O&M analytics** (distribution uniformity, water/energy savings, reporting) | **FarmTwin** |
| Project management, client/government interface | **Lead Partner** (FarmTwin supports technical) |
| Liaison with **IIT Palakkad / KAU** for validation | **FarmTwin** |

### 3. Roles & responsibilities
- Each Party shall perform its scope with due skill, in line with the tender/contract and
  applicable BIS/ISO/FAO standards, and provide qualified personnel.
- The Lead Partner shall be the **prime contractor** to the government client unless the
  tender permits a formal JV/consortium; FarmTwin acts as **named technology sub-contractor**.
- Neither Party may bind the other beyond this MoU without written consent.

### 4. Commercials (to be detailed in a definitive Sub-Contract / Work Order)
- FarmTwin's compensation for the technology scope: **[₹ per ha / lump sum / % of contract value]**,
  e.g. design ~**₹[3–6]k/ha**, twin+IoT at cost-plus **[__]%**, monitoring SaaS **₹[1,000–1,500]/ha/yr**.
- Payment terms: **[milestone-linked / back-to-back with client payments]**.
- Taxes (GST) extra as applicable; each Party bears its own statutory dues.
- EMD / Performance Bank Guarantee for the prime bid: arranged by **Lead Partner**;
  cost-sharing (if any) per clause **[__]**.

### 5. Intellectual property
- FarmTwin retains **sole ownership** of the FarmTwin Engine, Studio, Runtime, source code,
  models, algorithms, trained parameters and brand. The Lead Partner receives a limited,
  non-exclusive licence to use deliverables **only** for the specific project.
- Project-specific survey/field data is shared for the project; FarmTwin may use **anonymised/
  aggregated** field data to improve its models (learning loop), per clause 6.
- No transfer or reverse-engineering of FarmTwin software is permitted.

### 6. Data & confidentiality
- Each Party keeps the other's confidential information secret (term + **[3] years** after).
- Field telemetry, designs and pricing are confidential to the project.
- FarmTwin may retain anonymised operational data for product improvement and research
  (incl. with IIT Palakkad/KAU), with no disclosure of client-identifying information.

### 7. Term
Effective from the date above until **[12/24] months** or execution of a definitive agreement
for an awarded project, whichever is later; renewable by mutual written consent.

### 8. Representations
Each Party warrants it is duly constituted, holds the registrations it claims (class,
GST, Udyam/DPIIT etc.), and that information shared for eligibility is true.

### 9. Exclusivity (choose one)
- **Option A (non-exclusive):** Either Party may work with others, **except** that for the
  specific tenders listed in **Annexure-1** the Parties bid **only** together for **[__]** months.
- **Option B (exclusive for Moolathara):** For all Moolathara RBC / CMI packages during the
  term, the Parties collaborate exclusively with each other.

### 10. Non-solicitation
Neither Party shall solicit the other's employees or the named client relationship developed
jointly, for **[12]** months after termination.

### 11. Limitation of liability & indemnity
- Civil-works defects, site safety and statutory site liabilities: **Lead Partner**.
- Defects attributable to FarmTwin's software/hardware scope: **FarmTwin**, capped at
  **[fees received for that scope]** (no indirect/consequential losses).

### 12. Termination
Either Party may terminate on **[30]** days' written notice, or immediately for material
breach uncured in **[15]** days or insolvency. Accrued rights and clauses 5, 6, 10, 11, 13
survive.

### 13. Dispute resolution & governing law
Governed by the laws of India; courts/arbitration seat at **[Palakkad / Ernakulam], Kerala**.
Disputes first by good-faith negotiation, then arbitration under the Arbitration and
Conciliation Act, 1996 (sole arbitrator, English).

### 14. General
This MoU records intent and the framework for a definitive agreement; clauses 5, 6, 10, 11,
13 are **binding**, the rest are **non-binding** statements of intent. Amendments in writing.
Counterparts and electronic signatures permitted.

## Part B — Vendor / empanelment registration checklist

### B1. Entity & statutory foundation (do these first)
- [ ] **Incorporate** the company (Pvt Ltd recommended for tenders & IP) — MCA/SPICe+.
- [ ] **PAN + TAN** of the entity.
- [ ] **GST registration** (Kerala).
- [ ] **Udyam (MSME)** registration — needed for MSME tender benefits.
- [ ] **DPIIT Startup India** recognition — unlocks startup procurement relaxations.
- [ ] **Bank account + cancelled cheque**; banker's solvency certificate (some tenders).
- [ ] **Digital Signature Certificate (DSC, Class-3)** for the authorised signatory (required
      for all Kerala/GeM e-tendering).
- [ ] **Professional/technical profile**: CVs, FarmTwin Engine/MVP, docs dossier, any
      IIT Palakkad/KAU letters, the 15-acre pilot as a reference site.

### B2. Government e-procurement portals to register on
- [ ] **Kerala e-Tenders / e-GP** (`etenders.kerala.gov.in`) — primary portal for KIIDC / WRD /
      Irrigation Dept / KIIFB-routed tenders. Register vendor profile + DSC.
- [ ] **GeM (Government e-Marketplace)** (`gem.gov.in`) — for software/SaaS, sensors, IoT,
      AMC/monitoring services sold directly to govt buyers.
- [ ] **KIIDC vendor/contractor registration** — check KIIDC's empanelment route for
      consultants/technology vendors (DPR, design, monitoring scopes).
- [ ] **Irrigation Dept / WRD Kerala contractor registration** — class-based; FarmTwin likely
      qualifies only for **consultancy/technology** categories, not civil classes → partner
      for civil (see Part A).
- [ ] **CPPP (`eprocure.gov.in`)** and **PMKSY/PDMC state nodal agency** lists — for
      micro-irrigation empanelled-vendor schemes (often state agri/horticulture dept run).
- [ ] **As a Micro-Irrigation System (MIS) supplier**, check the **PDMC empanelment** of the
      Kerala State Horticulture Mission / Agri Dept (drip/sprinkler vendor lists) — partner
      with an empanelled MIS company if direct empanelment needs BIS-certified product lines.

### B3. Documents to keep ready (typical tender pack)
- [ ] Certificate of incorporation, MoA/AoA, board resolution / PoA for signatory.
- [ ] PAN, GST, Udyam, DPIIT certificates.
- [ ] Audited financials / ITRs for last **[2–3]** years (new startup → see B4 relaxations).
- [ ] Turnover & net-worth certificates (CA-signed).
- [ ] Work-experience / completion certificates (use pilot + any paid pilots; else partner's).
- [ ] EMD instrument (DD/BG) or EMD-exemption certificate (DPIIT/MSME) where allowed.
- [ ] Technical compliance sheet, OEM/authorisation letters (for any third-party hardware).
- [ ] ISO/BIS/test certificates for supplied instruments (or supplier's, via MoU).
- [ ] Non-blacklisting self-declaration, GST/PF/ESI compliance declarations.

### B4. Startup / MSME relaxations to claim (verify per NIT)
- [ ] **EMD exemption** — DPIIT-recognised startups & MSEs are commonly exempt from Earnest
      Money Deposit (attach DPIIT/Udyam certificate).
- [ ] **Prior-turnover & prior-experience relaxation** — Indian govt procurement policy lets
      DPIIT startups bid without the usual turnover/experience floors (quality criteria still
      apply). Kerala has parallel startup-procurement support via **KSUM** — confirm current
      GO and whether it covers KIIDC/WRD tenders.
- [ ] **MSME purchase-preference / price-preference** where notified.
- [ ] **Tender-fee exemption** for startups/MSEs on several portals.
> Action: get a one-page note from KSUM on which procurement relaxations apply to KIIFB/CMI
> tenders, and carry the GO references in every bid.

### B5. Eligibility-gap strategy (because we're new + software-only)
- Civil class & big-turnover bars → **bid via the MoU partner** (Part A); FarmTwin is the
  named technology sub-contractor/consultant.
- For **pure technology scopes** (DPR/design, digital twin, monitoring SaaS, sensor supply)
  → bid **directly** on GeM / consultancy tenders using startup relaxations.
- Build a track record fast: KSUM-funded **15-acre pilot** + one Moolathara CMI zone →
  generates the **completion certificate** that unlocks larger bids.
- Line up **2–3 MIS/civil partners** (avoid single-partner dependence; keeps clause 9 flexible).

### B6. Target packages to watch (refresh from portals)
- [ ] Moolathara RBC **Phase-II** civil/CMI zones (post Phase-I commissioning).
- [ ] Phase-I **O&M / performance-monitoring** contracts (our sweet spot).
- [ ] **CMI DPR / design** packages (KIIDC) — sub-consultant route.
- [ ] Chitturpuzha command-area modernisation packages.
- [ ] State PDMC micro-irrigation cluster tenders (Palakkad districts).
