# 08 — FPO Pilot MoU (Template)

A lightweight, non-binding pilot MoU to sign with a Chittur-cluster FPO/FPC.
A signed MoU is the single most valuable traction artifact for the KSUM Idea
Grant pitch, AgriNext matchmaking, and the Seed Fund "purchase order" basis.

> Replace all `<...>` placeholders. Have it reviewed by a CA/CS before signing.
> This template is not legal advice.

---

## Memorandum of Understanding — KrishiTwin Pilot Deployment

**Between:**
KrishiTwin Technologies Private Limited (the "Company"), registered office at
`<address, Kerala>`, represented by `<Founder name>`, Director;

**And:**
`<FPO/FPC legal name>` (the "FPO"), registered at `<address>`, represented by
`<name, designation>`.

Effective date: `<date>` · Pilot duration: `<3 / 6>` months.

### 1. Purpose
To pilot the Company's farm digital-twin and precision-irrigation platform with
`<N>` member farmers of the FPO in the `<Chittur>` cluster, to demonstrate water
savings and protected/improved yield.

### 2. Scope
- The Company will: deploy the platform for `<N>` farms, configure the zone mesh,
  provide daily irrigation/fertigation recommendations, and an FPO cluster
  dashboard; train FPO staff; provide support during the pilot.
- The FPO will: nominate participating farmers and a coordinator, share basic
  plot/crop/soil data, facilitate field access, and record irrigation events and
  harvest outcomes.

### 3. Data
- Farmer/field data shared for the pilot is used only to operate and validate the
  platform; aggregated/anonymized insights may be used by the Company to improve
  models and for reporting to KSUM/AgriNext. No farmer data is sold during the
  pilot. (Phase-2 data products require a separate, explicit agreement — doc 09.)

### 4. Commercials
- The pilot is provided **free of charge** / at a nominal fee of `<Rs.____>`.
- Post-pilot pricing intent: `<Rs. per farmer per season>` SaaS via the FPO,
  subject to a separate commercial agreement if the pilot meets success metrics.

### 5. Success metrics (jointly measured)
- >= `<30>`% reduction in water applied vs the farmer's baseline schedule.
- Yield maintained or improved vs baseline blocks.
- Soil-moisture model tracking sensor readings within `<±15>`%.

### 6. Sensors / equipment
Ownership and handling of any sensors deployed: `<Company-owned, returnable>` /
`<funded under Idea Grant / AgriNext>`.

### 7. Nature of MoU
This MoU records intent to collaborate on a pilot and is **non-binding** except
for the Data (Sec. 3) and Confidentiality (Sec. 8) clauses. Either party may
withdraw with `<15>` days' written notice.

### 8. Confidentiality
Each party will keep the other's non-public information confidential.

### 9. Governing law
This MoU is governed by the laws of India; courts at `<Palakkad, Kerala>`.

**Signed:**

For KrishiTwin Technologies Pvt Ltd          For `<FPO name>`
`<name / designation / date>`                `<name / designation / date>`
