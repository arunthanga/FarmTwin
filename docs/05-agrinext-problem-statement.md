# 05 — AgriNext / KERA Problem-Statement & Startup Submission

AgriNext (under the World Bank-backed KERA project, run by KSUM) connects
agri-startups with farmers and FPOs, offers funding up to **Rs.25 lakh**, and
provides deployment pathways to 40,000+ farmers. It collects **farmer problem
statements** and invites startups to submit solutions.
Portal: <https://kera.startupmission.in/> ; Summit: <http://agrinextsummit.startupmission.in/>

There are two complementary things to submit. Both are drafted below.

---

## A. Problem statement (file it as a farmer/cluster need)

**Title:** Need-based irrigation & water budgeting for the Chittur rain-shadow
vegetable/mango cluster.

**Cluster:** Eruthempathy, Kozhinjampara, Vadakarapathy, Perumatty (Chittur,
Palakkad). ~1,500 vegetable farmers, ~650 ha; new Moolathara canal water.

**Problem:** Newly available canal water is applied by habit, not by need.
Over-/under-irrigation wastes 30-50% of scarce water and costs 10-25% of yield.
FPOs lack any tool to budget water across the cluster or stagger harvest to the
Nallepilly market.

**Desired outcome:** A plot-level, day-by-day irrigation/fertigation decision
tool + an FPO cluster dashboard for water budgeting and harvest planning, proven
to cut water use >=30% at protected yield.

**Why it matters to KERA:** Directly serves KERA's "Climate Resilience &
Precision Farming" and "Precision Nutrient & Fertilizer Management" goals, and
strengthens FPOs as the adoption anchor.

---

## B. Startup solution submission

**Startup:** FarmTwin — simulation-driven farm digital twin (see docs 01, 02).

**What we deploy in the pilot:**
1. Discretize each member farm into an irrigation zone **mesh**.
2. Run the water-balance + crop-growth solver daily using weather/ET + soil
   sensors to issue per-zone irrigation/fertigation recommendations.
3. FPO dashboard: cluster water budget, stress map, staggered-harvest planner.
4. Scenario engine for climate-resilience planning (heat wave, dry spell).

**Fit to AgriNext asks:**
- Ready to run a real on-ground pilot (founder's farm + FPO members).
- Hardware-light, affordable, scalable -> meets "proven, affordable, scalable".
- Produces measurable outcomes (water saved, yield protected) for MoUs.

**Ask from AgriNext:**
- Pilot funding (up to Rs.25 lakh) for cluster-scale deployment.
- Matchmaking with a Chittur FPO/FPC and a signed pilot MoU (template doc 08).
- Inclusion in the AgriNext deployment/procurement pathway.
- Introduction to Agriculture Department / Krishi Bhavan buyers for post-pilot
  **GAAM direct procurement** (up to Rs.50 lakh ex-GST without tender under
  GO Ms No 2/2022/SPD once KSUM-registered).

**Founder edge:** 20 years of CAE/simulation (meshing, assembly-line sim) — the
exact competency a *digital twin* requires — plus a 15-acre pilot farm inside the
target cluster.
