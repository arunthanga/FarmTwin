## A. The 3-minute spoken pitch (emotional + professional)

> Deliver slowly. Pause at the blank lines. Aim ~430-470 words.

Judges, let me start with a queue.

Not a queue for a product launch — a queue of farmers in my village, **Eruthempathy**,
standing for hours in 40-degree heat at the Kunnamattupathy regulator, waiting for a tanker
to bring the day's drinking water. We are farmers. And we run out of water.

My region, Chittur in Palakkad, is Kerala's rain shadow. We get **less than a third** of the
rain the rest of the state gets. I grew up watching coconut palms wither, and watching the
young leave because the soil couldn't pay them back.

For twenty years, I left too — but to build software. CAE simulation: meshing algorithms,
virtual assembly lines, digital twins of factories, so the world's manufacturers could test
a production line *before* spending a rupee on steel.

And one day it hit me. **I could simulate a car factory to the millimetre — but my own
village couldn't predict whether water would reach a field next week.**

So I came home to build **FarmTwin**: a digital twin for irrigation. It does two things.
*Before* installation, it surveys a farm and designs the optimal system — pumps, pipes,
drips, sensors — and shows the best two or three layouts. *After* installation, it runs the
farm: solar-powered, wireless controllers read the soil and the weather and decide exactly
when and how much to water and fertilise. And it **learns** — every season it gets more
accurate.

Now here is why this is not a someday story. **It is happening, two kilometres from my
house, right now.** Kerala's **largest community micro-irrigation project** — the Moolathara
Right Bank Canal extension, two hundred and sixty crore rupees — is being commissioned this
year, and it names my panchayat, Eruthempathy, as a beneficiary. The civil tenders are
live.

But I know my region's history. We have been given canals before. The last lift canal has
had **no summer water for seven years**. A clean-up was announced, the money vanished, the
coconuts died. **Concrete is not the same as water in the roots.**

That gap — making the system actually deliver, and *proving* it drop by drop — is exactly
what FarmTwin does. Our immediate win is to be the **design and digital-twin brain** of this
Moolathara project. Our pilot is my own 15 acres. And from this one canal, the same software
scales to Chitturpuzha, to Kerala, to India's seventy million hectares — to farmers
everywhere.

I spent twenty years simulating the world's factories. With your support, I want to spend
the next twenty making sure **no farmer in my village — or anyone's village — has to stand
in that queue again.**

That's FarmTwin. Thank you.

## B. One-pager (leave-behind)

**FarmTwin — a self-calibrating digital twin for irrigation**
*Design the system right. Run it smart. Prove every drop.*

**Problem.** In Palakkad's rain-shadow belt (and across water-stressed India), irrigation
infrastructure is built but **doesn't reach the last farmer's roots** — Moolathara's old
canal has had no summer water for 7 years; ₹2 cr of clean-up vanished; 1,000+ acres hit.
The last mile is an **information & control** problem, not a concrete one.

**Solution.** Two products on one learning engine:
- **FarmTwin Studio** — pre-install survey → optimised design (pumps, pipes, valves, drips,
  sensors, fertigation) → best 2-3 layouts by cost/energy/uniformity/yield.
- **FarmTwin Runtime** — solar, wireless edge controllers that sense + decide + act on
  pumps/valves/fertigation, with bad-data guards and a digital twin that recalibrates and
  improves accuracy each season.

**Immediate win (de-risked, local, funded).** The **Moolathara RBC extension** — Kerala's
largest community micro-irrigation project (~₹262 cr, KIIFB), commissioning 2026, serving
**3,575 ha** (Phase I) and **10,000+ ha** (Phase II) in our own panchayat — has **live CMI
tenders**. FarmTwin wins the **design + digital-twin + performance-monitoring** layer
(with a civil partner) and a KSUM-funded pilot on 15 owned acres.

**Opportunity.** SOM ₹1-2 cr (Yr 1-2) → beachhead ₹10-25 cr (Chitturpuzha) → SAM ₹100-300
cr (Kerala CMI/PDMC) → TAM very large (India PMKSY ₹1000s cr/yr; ~70 M ha; global precision
irrigation). *All [est.] except cited facts; see doc 12.*

**Why us.** 20 yrs CAE simulation; working open engine + MVP (github.com/arunthanga/
FarmTwin); 15-acre pilot in the target panchayat; IIT Palakkad + KAU validation.

**Model.** Design fee + IoT/twin supply (fundable inside ~85% micro-irrigation subsidy) +
recurring SaaS/O&M (₹1-1.5k/ha/yr). Phase-2: yield-backed agri-fintech.

**Ask (KSUM).** Grant to build the field controller + sensor kit, run the calibrated pilot,
validate with IIT-PKD/KAU, and win the first Moolathara CMI design/monitoring contract.

## C. Elevator lines (pick by context)

- **5-second:** "FarmTwin is a digital twin that makes sure every drop in an irrigation
  canal actually reaches the farmer's roots — and proves it."
- **15-second:** "I spent 20 years building factory simulations. FarmTwin brings that to
  agriculture: software that designs a farm's irrigation, then runs it with solar wireless
  controllers — starting with Kerala's biggest micro-irrigation canal, in my own village."
- **For investors:** "Government is pouring crores into micro-irrigation canals that
  historically fail at the last mile. We're the intelligence + monitoring layer that makes
  them work — beachhead at Moolathara (₹262 cr project, live tenders), scaling to India's
  70 million hectares."
- **For government/KIIDC:** "We design the CMI zones, run them as a digital twin, and give
  you live proof of water-saving and delivery — so this canal doesn't become the next one
  that was built but never delivered."
- **For farmers (Malayalam-ready):** "നിങ്ങളുടെ കണ്ണിലെ വെള്ളം വേരിൽ എത്തുന്നു എന്ന് ഉറപ്പാക്കുന്ന
  സംവിധാനം." ("A system that makes sure the water meant for you actually reaches your crop's
  roots.")

## D. Demo-day checklist
- Open with the queue story (Slide 2) — earn attention before data.
- Show the **MVP** live (farm digital-twin simulator) for 30 seconds.
- Put the **Moolathara map** on screen: "₹262 cr, live tenders, my panchayat."
- One KPI promise: "Phase I targets up to **70% less water** — we make sure it's real."
- Close with the factories → farmers line.
